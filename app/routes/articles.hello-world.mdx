## Replace this article

This file is a starter article for your portfolio. Rewrite it with your own story,
technical breakdown, project retrospective, or learning notes.

## A simple structure that works well

1. Start with the problem or topic.
2. Explain your approach and decisions.
3. Share the result, lesson, or takeaway.

## Keep it practical

Short articles are enough. A clear point of view and a few concrete details usually
matter more than writing something long.
